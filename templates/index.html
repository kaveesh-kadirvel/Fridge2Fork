How It Works

Fridge2Fork turns pantry chaos into dinner plans using explainable matching and a curated recipe dataset.

1

Collect

Enter ingredients you have. We normalize and tokenize quickly for speed and clarity.

2

Match

We compute overlap between your tokens and each recipe. A clear match % shows why a recipe ranks.

3

Present

We present beautiful cards with images and full steps. Click any card for detailed view.

Backend: Python Flask APIs: `/api/recipes`, `/api/recipe/:id`.
Dataset: CSV with titles, ingredients, instructions, image names.
Ranking: Tokenization + overlap score ‚Üí match percentage.
Images: Local `static/img` or semantic Unsplash Featured fallback.
Frontend: Vanilla JS + semantic HTML + custom CSS.
